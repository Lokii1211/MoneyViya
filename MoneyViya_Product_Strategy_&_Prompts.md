### The Psychology of Financial Behavior Change

Understanding WHY people fail at finance = Understanding how MoneyViya wins:

**Failure Mode 1: Abstraction**
- Numbers are abstract. ₹500 saved doesn't feel real.
- **MoneyViya Fix:** Always connect money to dreams. "₹500 saved = 1 day closer to Goa trip"

**Failure Mode 2: Loss Aversion**
- Tracking expenses feels like punishment.
- **MoneyViya Fix:** Frame everything as wins. Never say "you overspent." Say "here's how to win tomorrow."

**Failure Mode 3: Complexity Paralysis**
- Investment options are overwhelming.
- **MoneyViya Fix:** Never give more than ONE recommendation at a time. One action per message.

**Failure Mode 4: Forgetting**
- Life happens. Finance gets forgotten.
- **MoneyViya Fix:** Be part of their WhatsApp routine. Morning. Evening. Celebrations.

**Failure Mode 5: Shame**
- People don't want to admit financial failure.
- **MoneyViya Fix:** Zero judgment. Always forward-looking. Never shame data.
