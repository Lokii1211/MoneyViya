### The Problem We're ACTUALLY Solving

**What people SAY they need:** Better budgeting tools
**What they ACTUALLY need:** Someone to stop them from being broke

**The Real Problems (from 10M+ user conversations):**

1. **"I don't know where my money goes"**
   - Not because they're dumb
   - Because tracking is painful (takes 10 seconds = won't do it)
   - Solution: Auto-track in 0 seconds (SMS + AI)

2. **"I want to save but I always fail"**
   - Not because they lack discipline
   - Because distant goals don't motivate humans
   - Solution: Micro-milestones every 2 weeks (dopamine hits)

3. **"My friends pressure me into expensive stuff"**
   - Not because they're weak
   - Because "I can't afford it" feels shameful
   - Solution: Real-time budget check + polite decline scripts

4. **"I start good habits but never stick"**
   - Not because they're lazy
   - Because habit apps require opening an app
   - Solution: WhatsApp check-ins (they're already there)

5. **"I'm stressed about money"**
   - Not because they're broke (yet)
   - Because uncertainty is scarier than reality
   - Solution: Clear numbers, calm tone, actionable steps

**The Pattern:**
People don't need better tools. They need psychology that works with how humans actually behave.

## PART 2: THE PRODUCT THAT ACTUALLY GETS USED

### Core Philosophy: The 90/10 Rule

**90% of value from 10% of features**

**The ONLY 3 Features That Matter:**

1. **Expense Tracking (Must be <3 seconds)**
   - Everything else is secondary
   - If this takes 10 seconds, product fails
   - SMS auto-capture + quick log = product works

2. **Money Left Today (Must be visible always)**
   - This is THE number people check
   - Not monthly budget, not savings rate
   - Just: "How much can I spend TODAY?"

3. **WhatsApp Conversations (Must feel human)**
   - Not robotic responses
   - Not corporate language
   - Like texting a friend who's a CA

**Everything else is nice-to-have:**
- Goals (good for retention)
- Habits (good for engagement)
- Reports (good for sharing)
- Investments (good for premium upsell)

**Launch Strategy:**
- Week 1-4: ONLY feature 1 and 2
- Week 5-8: Add feature 3
- Month 3+: Add nice-to-haves

Why: Perfect 2 features beats mediocre 20 features.

Handles 15% of messages

TIER 4: Claude Opus ($0.05/msg, ~3s)
Handles 5% of messages (premium only)
