# Viya — Play Store & App Store Listing

## App Name
Viya — Your Life, Automated

## Short Description (80 chars)
AI-powered money, health & habit tracker. Your life operating system. 🚀

## Full Description

**Viya is the only app you need to run your entire life** — money, health, habits, and productivity — all powered by AI.

🏦 **SMART MONEY MANAGEMENT**
• Auto-track expenses from bank SMS — zero manual entry
• Smart budgets with AI overspending alerts  
• Bill reminders so you never miss a due date
• Subscription tracker — find hidden recurring charges
• Split bills with friends in one tap
• Investment tracking & SIP calculator

💚 **HEALTH & WELLNESS HUB**  
• Weight, water, mood & sleep tracking
• Medicine reminder with push notifications
• Meal logger with calorie estimation
• AI mood analysis from journal entries
• Sleep quality scoring

🔥 **HABIT BUILDER**
• Build any habit with beautiful streak tracking
• Streak-freeze tokens to protect your progress
• 7-day visual streak calendar
• Smart celebratory animations on milestones
• 50+ achievement badges to unlock

🤖 **WHATSAPP AI ASSISTANT**
• Just text "spent 500 on food" to log expenses
• Ask "how much left?" for instant budget status
• Get daily morning briefs & weekly summaries
• Voice note support — speak your expenses
• Receipt scanning — snap & auto-log

🏆 **GAMIFICATION**
• Earn XP for every action — level up from Beginner to Legend
• Weekly challenges: No-Spend Weekend, 10K Steps, and more
• Compete with friends on the leaderboard
• Unlock 50+ badges and custom rewards

📊 **AI INSIGHTS & PREDICTIONS**
• AI analyzes your spending patterns
• Predicts month-end spending before it happens
• Personalized savings tips
• Monthly AI report card with grades

**WHY VIYA?**
✅ No bank login needed — works from SMS
✅ WhatsApp bot included — no app switching
✅ Beautiful dark UI — feels native, not web
✅ 100% free to start
✅ Made in India 🇮🇳
